About Me

I'm a 31 year old living in Center City, Philadelphia with my wife and two dogs. I grew up in Chicago, Frankfurt Germany, and Northern Virginia. I played Division 1 baseball at Mount Saint Mary's University in Emmitsburg, Maryland from 2007-2011 (here's my bio). Once I realized I couldn't hit a curveball and wouldn't make it as a pro, I decided to go to law school.

I was the first hire at a youth sports sponsorship startup in Philly and previously co-founded a peer-to-peer editing platform (it no longer exists...turns out people don't like things edited).

I'm also an obsessive hobbyist. I am a part-time professional poker player, a Class-A chess player (follow my journey to master here!), an amateur card magician, and occasionally a writer (here are some articles from the past). I'm always looking to meet new people and experience new things.
