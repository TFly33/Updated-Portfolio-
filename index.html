About Me

I'm a 31 year old living in Center City, Philadelphia with my wife and two dogs. I grew up in Chicago, Frankfurt Germany, and Northern Virginia. I played played Division 1 baseball during college. (here's my bio). Once I realized I couldn't hit a curveball, I decided to go to law school.

I graduated from law school in 2014. I'm barred to practice law in both Virginia and Pennsylvania. I've been working as an associate attorney since 2014 and currently specialize in real estate law. After moving to Philadelphia, I started a real estate investment company. We currently own and operate about twenty units total in Philadelphia and Baltimore. I also have some startup experience. I was the first hire at a youth sports sponsorship startup in Philly and previously co-founded a peer-to-peer editing platform (it no longer exists...turns out people don't like things edited).

I'm also an obsessive hobbyist. I am a part-time professional poker player, a Class-A chess player (follow my journey to master here!), an amateur card magician, and occasionally a writer (here are some articles from the past). I'm always looking to meet new people and experience new things.
